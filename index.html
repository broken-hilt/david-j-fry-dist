Original paintings by Herefordshire artist David Fry

I am a professional artist living in Herefordshire, England UK.

The majority of my work is of the Herefordshire landscape, not just the rivers and streams but buildings in villages and towns, capturing the character and style of bygone architecture. Several of my paintings, with timber framed houses and buildings of immense beauty have been produced from old black and white photographs, as the scenes depicted no longer exist.

I have had my paintings exhibited in over sixty venues as well as having prints and cards on permanent display in shops and galleries, and now I am able to offer my work via this website.
