Limited Edition Prints

Authenticity

Each limited edition print is accompanied by a certificate of authenticity. Both the print and certificate are embossed, numbered and signed by the artist.

Materials

The prints have been produced using acid free card and light fast pigment inks.

Number of Copies and Dimensions

The vast majority of the limited edition prints have been restricted to ninety five copies. They vary in size due to the different dimensions of the original paintings, but with an approximate measurement of a two inch mount around the print, it will fit in a frame of 14"x 11".

Print Ref No. 714 The Cathedrals of Hereford, Gloucester & Worcester is however, restricted to just sixty five copies. It has an image size of 11.5"x 8", and will fit in a 16"x 12", frame.
